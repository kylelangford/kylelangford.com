Bally Ribbon Mills

Site Design and Drupal Development

Drupal site design and development.

Ask Bally Ribbon Mills produces a wide variety of narrow woven fabrics, webbing, tapes and custom fabrication. They do confidential work for the government but also have public clients like NASA. (Sidenote: They made the descent parachute for one of the Mars rovers). Bally wanted a Drupal website to manage and showcase their product information and capabilities.

Outcome This was my first time as the design and development lead for a web project at Brunello. I worked with Adam Hoyle (Owner) to give Bally an authoritative and clearly laid out site that made connecting with clients easy compared to competitors. I produced the design work which included sitemaps, wireframes, moodboards, and full-page mockups. Site development was done by me using Drupal 7 CMS.
