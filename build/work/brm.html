Bally Ribbon Mills

Site Design and Drupal Development

Drupal Site Design and Development.

Ask Bally Ribbon Mills produces a wide variety of narrow woven fabrics, webbing, tapes and custom fabrication. They do confidential work for the government but also have public clients like NASA. (Sidenote: They made the descent parachute for one of the Mars rovers). Bally wanted a Drupal website to manage and showcase their product information and capabilities.

Contribution I was initialy hired by Brunello to do soup to nuts web sites. I did several of these but the those most successful was Bally Ribbon Mills. I worked with Adam Hoyle (Project Manager) to give Bally an authoritative and clearly laid out site that made connecting with clients easy compared to competitors. I produced the design work which included sitemaps, wireframes, moodboards, and full-page mockups. Site development was done by me using Drupal 7 CMS.
