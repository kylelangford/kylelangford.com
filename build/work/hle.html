Healthly Living Engine

UI Design and Frontend Development

Healthy Content Through your Healthplan

Ask A centralized content publishing platform for recipes, healthy articles, and coupons. The Platform needed to provide hosted microsites that can be customized in look and content. Content was targeted at healthcare plans looking to supplement their digitial communications and benefits. The Platform also needed to provide a suite of content creation tools, such as lineups, reporting, workflows, and project tracking.

Outcome We created a powerful Drupal API and Multisite application that could handle the changing demands of a content publishing platform. We designed a flexible theme that is easily customizable and updatable. Content can be packaged and sent to microsites and controlled from a centralized point. We designed easy to use custom backend themes to create content and reports. We continue to develop features and push the product into the future.
