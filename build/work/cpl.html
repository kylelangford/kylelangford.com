Cambridge Public Library

Wayfinding and Signage

Reverse channel letters on canopy.

Enviromental Signage.

Ask The Cambridge Public Library wanted to create a new interior and exterior signage system for the newly renovated building and new building section.

Outcome I worked as the Project Designer with the Principal Designer (Tom) to conceptualize and design the signage and wayfinding of the building using architectural drawings and virtual walkthroughs as references. This included everything from welcome signs, floor maps, restroom signs, even the numbers on the elevator jams. I choose the typography, photography, and visual styles used for the project. I drafted production ready artwork to send to fabrication, this included designing mounts, selecting materials, and demonstrating exact sign placement. I also created 3D/2D visualizations to present design work to the client for review.
