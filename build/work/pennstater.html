Design and Generate HTML Emails

Ask Drizly needed help designing and producing emails in order to drive continuous traffic to their alcohol ordering web service. My partner and I consulted with them for some time, designing a reoccurring email and also helping code emails.

Outcome There was a lot of commonalities in the emails we built. I proposed that we design a library of reusable templates based on their existing email designs. We boiled the emails down to a dozen or so components. I designed and built a UI for arranging these templates and populating them with content. This app would render the HTML in a preview panel and also allows you to export the generated HTML, which we would place into Litmus and eventually into ExactTarget.
