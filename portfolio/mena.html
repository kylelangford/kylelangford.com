Mass. Emergency Nurse Association

Site Design and Build

Labor of Love

The Mass. Emergency Nurse Association is the state chapter of the national organization. They needed a website to post information and organize events. There was a knowledge gap in this project so I decided the best way to show design was to build it. I utilized mood-boards to show look and feel then implemented the remaining features based on that.
