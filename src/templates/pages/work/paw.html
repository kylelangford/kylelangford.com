Digital Magazine Publishing

My accomplishments for this project include crafting a process from research to development. I designed reusable components and list styles that were used to layout all the pages of the site. I was responsible for creating the sitemap, mobile-priority, layout wireframes, and working prototype. I also produced all the front-end production code, much of which was recycled from the prototype.

Princeton Alumni Website, prior to redesign.

We wanted to add more of the magazines editorial look and feel to the online experience.

Content Priority
I created this Content Inventory — to show at a block level the heirarchy of information for the page. We created a list of all possible blocks and used that to scaffold out all the page types. Types were assigned to blocks of similar functionality to display placements.
