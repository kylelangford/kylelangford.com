Angular Video Player

Elev8d Fitness app provided users with guided workout playlists as well as full-length video workouts. This was a webview app so that presented a lot of challenges with video playback. We were able to design a player that simplified the UI and allowed seamless playing. I focused on transitions and animation to give a more native app feel.
