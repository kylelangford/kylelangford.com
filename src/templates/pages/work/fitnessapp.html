HTML5 Video Magic

Elev8d Fitness app provided users with full-length video workouts as well as guided video playlists. The original Angular build was riddled with bugs and had a very confusing design. This was a webview app so that presented a lot of challenges with video playback. We were able to design a player that simplified the UI and allowed seamless playing. I focused on transitions and animation to give a more native app feel. This project was the first time I had worked with Angular v5 and typescript which made this challanging.
