Healthy Content Hub
{{picture "projects/hle/device-mockup-feature.png" "" "eager"}}
Plansite 2.0 Design By: Kyle Langford

Ask A centralized content publishing platform for recipes, healthy articles, and coupons. The content api feeds microsites that can be customized in look and content. The Platform also provides a suite of content creation tools, such as lineups, reporting, workflows, and project tracking. Originally called LMS this platform was built in Drupal 7. A major aspect of this project was to migrate to Drupal 8.

Outcome We designed a flexible theme that is easily customizable and updatable. Content can be packaged and sent to microsites and controlled from a centralized point. We designed and built backend themes and worflow tools to create content and reports. We continue to develop features and push the product into the future.
